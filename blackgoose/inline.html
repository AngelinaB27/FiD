Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited
about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food
(usually vacum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath is precisely
set to the target temperature you want the
food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Porter describes it as
ultra-low-temperature poaching.
